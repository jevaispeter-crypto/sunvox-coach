# **Energy and Dynamics**# ### \## What it isEnergy is how intensity evolves over time in music.It is created by combining:\- rhythm\- density\- pitch\- volume\- variation### \## Core IdeaMusic is not static.It should:\- build tension\- release tension### \## Main Energy Tools#### \### 1. DensityMore notes = more energy  Fewer notes = less energy#### \### 2. RegisterHigher notes feel more intense  Lower notes feel more grounded#### \### 3. RhythmFaster / denser rhythms increase energy  More space reduces energy#### \### 4. Repetition vs changeRepetition = stability  Change = movement / tension### \## Energy Shapes#### \### BuildGradually increasing:\- notes\- speed\- intensity#### #### \### Drop / ReleaseSudden simplification:\- silence\- fewer elements#### \### PlateauMaintaining constant energy### \## Common mistakeBeginners:\- keep constant energy\- or change too randomlyGood music:\- controls energy intentionally### \## In practice (SunVox)You control energy by:\- adding/removing patterns\- changing note density\- using higher/lower notes### \## Mental modelMusic = controlled energy over time
