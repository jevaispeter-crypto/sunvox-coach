Stereo Image and Panning

## Core idea
Stereo creates separation, not decoration.

## What the student must understand
- mono vs stereo
- panning
- center vs sides

## Why it matters
Weak production:
- everything centered
- masking

Strong production:
- clear separation
- balanced stereo

## Hearing model
Center → core elements  
Sides → support

## Practical interpretation
If mix feels:
- crowded → pan before EQ
- flat → add stereo contrast

## Production heuristics
- keep kick/bass centered
- pan supporting elements
- avoid random panning
- check mono compatibility

## Core drills
- center vs spread comparison
- pan conflicting sounds

## Common mistakes
- over-widening
- ignoring mono
- random panning

## In SunVox
- separate tracks spatially
- keep low-end centered
