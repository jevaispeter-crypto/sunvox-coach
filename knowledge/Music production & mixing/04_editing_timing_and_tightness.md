Editing, Timing, and Tightness

## Core idea
Mixing does not fix timing problems.
Timing must be correct before processing.

## What the student must understand
- timing alignment
- microtiming (ahead / behind)
- quantization vs groove
- transient alignment

## Why it matters
Weak production:
- misaligned layers
- inconsistent rhythm
- weak punch

Strong production:
- aligned transients
- consistent groove

## Hearing model
Aligned → punch  
Misaligned → smear

## Practical interpretation
If something feels:
- weak → check alignment
- messy → check timing

## Production heuristics
- fix timing before processing
- align kick & bass first
- avoid over-quantizing

## Core drills
- offset one sound → hear punch loss
- align vs misalign kick/bass
- strict vs loose timing

## Common mistakes
- ignoring timing
- stacking misaligned sounds
- over-quantizing
