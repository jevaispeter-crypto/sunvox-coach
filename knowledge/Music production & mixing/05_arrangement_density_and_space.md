Arrangement, Density, and Space

## Core idea
Clarity comes from arrangement, not processing.

## What the student must understand
- density
- layering vs clutter
- role separation
- overlap

## Why it matters
Weak production:
- too many elements
- masking
- clutter

Strong production:
- fewer, clearer elements
- defined roles

## Hearing model
More layers = more masking  
Silence = clarity

## Practical interpretation
If mix feels:
- crowded → too many sounds
- muddy → overlap

## Production heuristics
- remove before processing
- simplify competing sounds
- fewer strong > many weak

## Core drills
- mute layers → hear clarity
- stack vs separate sounds
- sparse vs dense comparison

## Common mistakes
- over-layering
- filling silence
- fixing arrangement with EQ

## In SunVox
- reduce patterns
- separate tracks
- use space intentionally
