# Sampler Module Guide

## Why Use Samples?

- Realistic instruments
- More expressive than generators

## Source

- freesound.org

## Licensing

- CC0 → best (no restrictions)
- Attribution → requires credit
- Non-commercial → avoid for selling

## Multi-Sample Principle

One sample is NOT enough

Problem:
- Pitch shifting distorts sound

Solution:
- Use multiple samples across pitch range

## Sampler Structure

- Slots = individual samples
- Banks = expanded capacity

## Polyphony

- Default: 8
- Recommended: 32

## Key Concept

Instrument quality = sample coverage + mapping
