# Timing System (CRITICAL)

## Core Formula

1 beat = 24 ticks

## Default

- 4 lines per beat
- 6 ticks per line

6 × 4 = 24 ticks

## Changing Timing

If you change:
- ticks per line
- or time grid

→ you must maintain 24 ticks per beat

## Time Grid

Controls visual spacing

Examples:
- 4 → standard
- 3 → triplet feel
- 5 → 5/4 alignment

## BPM Accuracy

If ticks ≠ 24 per beat:
→ BPM becomes inaccurate

## Advanced

You can redefine:
- what a “beat” means
- what a “note length” means

## Key Insight

SunVox timing is mathematical, not musical by default
