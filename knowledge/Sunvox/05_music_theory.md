# Music Theory (Applied to SunVox)

## Time Signature

- 4/4 = 4 beats per measure
- 5/4 = 5 beats per measure

## Tempo

- BPM = beats per minute

## Notes

- Half note = 2 beats
- Quarter = 1 beat
- Eighth = 1/2 beat

## Accidentals

- Sharp (#) → +1 semitone
- Flat (b) → -1 semitone
- Natural → cancel accidental

## SunVox Specific

- Uses sharps only (no flats)
- Flats converted to sharps internally

## Triplets

- 3 notes in space of 2
